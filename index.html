Our Story

Built brick by brick, since 2008.

Bihar Bricks Industry was established in 2008 by Indra Kumar Yadav with a simple goal: give builders in and around Darbhanga a dependable source of quality bricks, made the traditional way and fired for strength.

From our yard at Narsar, Sihaul, Baheri, we've grown into a trusted supplier for homes, boundary walls, and large construction projects — one order, and one relationship, at a time.

  • Kiln-fired for consistent strength
  • Direct-from-yard pricing, no middlemen
  • Reliable bulk delivery across Darbhanga

What We Supply

Every batch, sorted honestly by grade.

Not every brick fired in the kiln comes out the same — so we sort every batch and sell it by grade, at a price that matches its quality. No mixing, no surprises on site.

A

1 Number Bricks

Grade A — Top Quality

Fully and evenly fired, hardest and strongest of the lot. Best for foundations, load-bearing walls, and any work where strength matters most.

B

Mitha Paak

Grade B — Second Quality

Well-fired and solid, just a shade under Grade A. A reliable, cost-effective choice for general wall construction.

C

2 Number Bricks

Grade C — Third Quality

Lighter-fired bricks at a more economical rate. Commonly used for internal walls, filling work, and boundary construction.

D

Goriya Bricks

Grade D — Low Quality

Under-fired, softer bricks sold at the lowest full-brick rate. Suited to light, non-structural or temporary work.

×

Tuta Hua Brick

Broken Bricks

Broken pieces from the kiln, sold at the most economical rate — ideal for road filling, leveling, and foundation bedding.
